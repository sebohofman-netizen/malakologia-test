The Association of Polish Malacologists

The Association of Polish Malacologists (SMP) is a nationwide scientific and professional organisation bringing together researchers and enthusiasts of molluscs, both in Poland and abroad. By supporting the development of malacology, we promote scientific research, disseminate knowledge, and foster collaboration between academic and educational communities.

Our mission extends beyond advancing science to include environmental protection and raising awareness of the importance of molluscs in nature. We organise conferences, symposia, and scientific meetings, publish academic and popular materials — including the journal Folia Malacologica — and support the development of future generations of researchers.

We connect people, knowledge, and passion — creating a strong community of malacologists in Poland and worldwide.
